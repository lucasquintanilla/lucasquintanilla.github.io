About the courses

Have you ever felt overwhelmed by routine?
Wished for less screen time and more real connection?
Felt your creativity slipping away in the rush of everyday life?

In today’s fast-paced world, adults, teenagers, and children alike face constant pressure—from digital overload to performance anxiety and emotional burnout. It’s easy to lose touch with our inner voice and the joy of simply creating.

Our Music Summer Courses are designed to inspire creativity, self-expression, and enrich everyday life through music. In a calm, supportive space, each one-to-one session is a chance to slow down, reconnect, and grow—musically and personally.

We believe music is more than a skill—it’s a path to confidence, focus, and emotional wellbeing. Whether you're rediscovering a long-lost passion or stepping into something new, our tailored approach ensures that your experience feels meaningful, personal, and empowering.

Each journey is marked by a sense of accomplishment and creative discovery, with the option to capture your progress through recordings and receive a certificate to honour your commitment.

Piano Workshop Details

Explore the world of piano through fun, engaging one-on-one lessons tailored to each child's skill level. Whether your child is a complete beginner or has taken lessons before, this workshop offers a supportive space to build confidence, improve technique, and express creativity at the keyboard. Each student will work individually with an instructor, learning songs, rhythm, and musical expression at their own pace. They can choose to work with new material we provide or bring a piece they want help with—whether it's a favorite song, a school project, or an exam piece. At the end of the workshop, participants will showcase their progress with a professionally recorded audio and video performance of a piece they've learned. This workshop is also a great way for kids to explore their interest in piano and decide if they'd like to continue learning in the future.

Ages: 6–12
Level: Beginner to intermediate
What's provided: Keyboard, music materials, and personalized instruction, recorded audio visual of a song, workshop certificate

✅ What your child will gain from this workshop:

  • Learn to play songs they'll feel proud of
  • Explore a new hobby and musical skill
  • Take a healthy break from screens
  • Use music as a way to relax and self-regulate
  • Learn how to listen and focus
  • Become more organized and independent
  • Build confidence in themselves

Music Production Course Details

Step into the world of digital music creation! This one-on-one course introduces kids to the basics of music production using simple tools and software. Whether your child is brand new or already curious about beats and sounds, this is a fun and creative space to explore how music is made. Each student will work individually with an instructor, learning how to create rhythms, layer sounds, use virtual instruments, and arrange a complete track. They can follow the material we provide or bring a project or idea they want help developing—whether it's a school assignment, a beat they started, or a favorite song they want to recreate. At the end of the course, they'll leave with a professionally mixed audio track to share and keep. This is a great way for kids to explore music through technology and see if production is something they'd like to continue learning in the future.

Ages: 8–14
Level: Beginner to intermediate
What's provided: Music samples, personalized instruction, final audio track, screen-recorded video, course certificate
What's needed: Laptop or tablet (optional: headphones, midi controller)
Modality: In person or online

✅ What your child will gain from this course:

  • Create a song or beat they'll feel proud of
  • Explore a new hobby and digital skill
  • Take a healthy break from passive screen time
  • Use music as a creative and emotional outlet
  • Learn how to listen, edit, and refine their ideas
  • Practice problem-solving and independent thinking
  • Build confidence in their creativity

Music Theory / Ear Training Course Details

Give your child the tools to understand and hear music more clearly! This one-on-one course helps kids build a strong foundation in music theory and develop their musical ear in a fun, interactive way. Whether they're just starting out or want to strengthen their skills, this course supports deeper listening and smarter playing. Each student will work individually with an instructor to explore the basics of music theory—like notes, rhythm, scales, and chords—while also training their ears to recognize sounds, intervals, and patterns. Activities are hands-on, creative, and tailored to their level. They can work with the material we provide or bring theory topics they want help with—like preparing for an exam or improving their improvisation. At the end of the course, students will receive a personalized progress summary and digital practice tools to continue learning at home. This is a great way for kids to boost their musical understanding and grow more confident in any instrument or musical activity they pursue.

Ages: 8–14
Level: Beginner to early intermediate
What's provided: Personalized theory materials, listening tools, ear training apps/games, digital practice tools, course certificate

✅ What your child will gain from this course:

  • Understand how music works
  • Improve listening and musical memory
  • Explore a new skill that supports all instruments
  • Take a healthy break from screens
  • Use focused listening as a calming activity
  • Practice attention, structure, and self-discipline
  • Build confidence in their musical abilities

About Workshops

Our Summer Music Workshops offer an immersive experience for musicians of all levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced player wanting to refine your skills, our expert instructors will guide you through an enriching musical journey. Join us this summer for hands-on learning, collaborative performances, and unforgettable musical experiences.

All workshops take place in our state-of-the-art facilities with professional equipment and intimate class sizes to ensure personalized attention. Our programs run for 2 weeks with flexible scheduling options to accommodate your summer plans.

Detailed Workshop Information

🎹 Piano Workshop

Explore the world of piano through fun, engaging one-on-one lessons tailored to each child's skill level. Whether your child is a complete beginner or has taken lessons before, this workshop offers a supportive space to build confidence, improve technique, and express creativity at the keyboard. Each student will work individually with an instructor, learning songs, rhythm, and musical expression at their own pace. They can choose to work with new material we provide or bring a piece they want help with—whether it's a favorite song, a school project, or an exam piece. At the end of the workshop, participants will showcase their progress with a professionally recorded audio and video performance of a piece they've learned. This workshop is also a great way for kids to explore their interest in piano and decide if they'd like to continue learning in the future.

Ages: 6–12
Level: Beginner to intermediate
What's provided: Keyboard, music materials, and personalized instruction, recorded audio visual of a song, workshop certificate

✅ What your child will gain from this workshop:

  • Learn to play songs they'll feel proud of
  • Explore a new hobby and musical skill
  • Take a healthy break from screens
  • Use music as a way to relax and self-regulate
  • Learn how to listen and focus
  • Become more organized and independent
  • Build confidence in themselves

🎧 Music Production Workshop

Step into the world of digital music creation! This one-on-one workshop introduces kids to the basics of music production using simple tools and software. Whether your child is brand new or already curious about beats and sounds, this is a fun and creative space to explore how music is made. Each student will work individually with an instructor, learning how to create rhythms, layer sounds, use virtual instruments, and arrange a complete track. They can follow the material we provide or bring a project or idea they want help developing—whether it's a school assignment, a beat they started, or a favorite song they want to recreate. At the end of the workshop, they'll leave with a professionally mixed audio track to share and keep. This is a great way for kids to explore music through technology and see if production is something they'd like to continue learning in the future.

Ages: 8–14
Level: Beginner to intermediate
What's provided: Music samples, personalized instruction, final audio track, screen-recorded video, workshop certificate
What's needed: Laptop or tablet (optional: headphones, midi controller)
Modality: In person or online

✅ What your child will gain from this workshop:

  • Create a song or beat they'll feel proud of
  • Explore a new hobby and digital skill
  • Take a healthy break from passive screen time
  • Use music as a creative and emotional outlet
  • Learn how to listen, edit, and refine their ideas
  • Practice problem-solving and independent thinking
  • Build confidence in their creativity
All Levels 2 weeks

✍️ Songwriting Workshop

Turn thoughts and feelings into music! This one-on-one workshop helps kids explore the art of songwriting in a fun and supportive environment. Whether they're writing their first lyrics or already love making up songs, this is a space where their ideas can come to life. Each student will work individually with an instructor to create their own original song. They'll learn how to write lyrics, shape melodies, and build a song structure. They can start fresh with our guidance or bring in a song or idea they've already started—whether it's a school project, a journal entry, or just something they've been humming. At the end of the workshop, each student will record a demo version of their song (spoken or sung) to celebrate their work. This workshop is a great way for kids to express themselves through music and explore if songwriting is something they'd like to grow in.

Ages: 8–14
Level: Beginner to early intermediate
What's provided: Writing materials, recording tools, personalized instruction, demo recording, workshop certificate

✅ What your child will gain from this workshop:

  • Write a song they'll feel proud of
  • Explore a new hobby and creative skill
  • Take a healthy break from screens
  • Use songwriting as a way to express emotions
  • Learn how to listen to ideas and shape them into lyrics
  • Practice focus, structure, and self-motivation
  • Build confidence in their voice and creativity
All Levels 2 weeks

🎼 Composition Workshop

Turn musical ideas into original pieces! This one-on-one workshop helps kids explore the art of composing music in a fun and supportive environment. Whether they're just starting out or already love creating melodies, this is a space where their imagination can grow. Each student will work individually with an instructor to compose their own instrumental piece. They'll learn how to create melodies, build harmonies, write basic notation, and structure a full composition. They can start something new with our guidance or bring a piece they've already begun—whether it's for school, a personal project, or just for fun. At the end of the workshop, students will create a demo version of their composition (performed live or digitally) and receive a digital music sheet of their finished piece. This workshop is a great way for kids to express themselves through music and discover if composition is a path they'd like to follow further.

Ages: 8–14
Level: Beginner to early intermediate
What's provided: Writing and notation tools, recording and playback software, personalized instruction, demo recording, digital music sheet, workshop certificate

✅ What your child will gain from this workshop:

  • Compose a piece they'll feel proud of
  • Explore a new hobby and creative skill
  • Take a healthy break from screens
  • Use music to express ideas and emotions
  • Learn how to listen closely and organize musical thoughts
  • Practice focus, structure, and creative problem-solving
  • Build confidence in their musical voice
All Levels 2 weeks

👂 Music Theory / Ear Training Workshop

Give your child the tools to understand and hear music more clearly! This one-on-one workshop helps kids build a strong foundation in music theory and develop their musical ear in a fun, interactive way. Whether they're just starting out or want to strengthen their skills, this workshop supports deeper listening and smarter playing. Each student will work individually with an instructor to explore the basics of music theory—like notes, rhythm, scales, and chords—while also training their ears to recognize sounds, intervals, and patterns. Activities are hands-on, creative, and tailored to their level. They can work with the material we provide or bring theory topics they want help with—like preparing for an exam or improving their improvisation. At the end of the workshop, students will receive a personalized progress summary and digital practice tools to continue learning at home. This is a great way for kids to boost their musical understanding and grow more confident in any instrument or musical activity they pursue.

Ages: 8–14
Level: Beginner to early intermediate
What's provided: Personalized theory materials, listening tools, ear training apps/games, digital practice tools, workshop certificate

✅ What your child will gain from this workshop:

  • Understand how music works
  • Improve listening and musical memory
  • Explore a new skill that supports all instruments
  • Take a healthy break from screens
  • Use focused listening as a calming activity
  • Practice attention, structure, and self-discipline
  • Build confidence in their musical abilities
All Levels 2 weeks
